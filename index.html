Our Mission

The Art Show is a concept made to bring community back to clothing. We strive to create beautiful hand painted designs, while using high quality up-cycled clothing as our canvas. We want to create a space for the local art community to thrive, while providing something special and long lasting for our customers. Every person on this planet is one of a kind. We believe your clothes should be that way too.
